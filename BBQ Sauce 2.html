Lexington/Western Carolina Sauce

Vinegar with Tomato

How to Use It

Lexington or Western Carolina sauce is vinegar sauce with health amounts of tomato sauce, paste or ketchup added. While eastern Carolina sauce can be used as a baste, Lexington/West Carolina sauce is solely used as a "dip" for after the meet is cooked. Western Carolina is traditional used on the pork shoulder only.

The Best Places to Try It

Smiley's Barbeque in Lexington, NC; Little Richard's Bar-B-Que in Winston-Salem, NC; Buzz and Ned's Barbeque in Richmond, VA (oddly enough)
